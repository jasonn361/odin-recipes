Ichiraku Ramen

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort foor at Ichiraku Ramen, the hidden gem of the bustling Hidden Leaf Village. Customized for our lord 7th, our signature dish, the Naruto-style ramen, is a mouthwatering combination of miso ramen and pork bone broth, topped with tender slices of pork, green onions, and Naruto fish cake. Get a taste of what turned Naruto from a low-life brat into the most revered shinobi.
